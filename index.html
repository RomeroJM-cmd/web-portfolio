John Mark Romero

Full-Stack Developer

John Mark Romero, also known as "John" or "JM," was born on October 11, 2000, in Goa, Camarines Sur. Currently, he lives in Tanza, Cavite. He completed his junior and senior high school at Tanza National Trade School in Tanza, Cavite. He initially studied Drafting Technology but later switched to Computer Programming, focusing on the Technical Vocational track in K12. He graduated successfully and received his certificate in 2019. Driven by his passion for information technology, John decided to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Cavite State University-Indang. John aspires to become a Fullstack Web Developer. He aims to enhance his skills in design and programming, continuously improving and growing in these areas. He is eager to explore the dynamic and ever-evolving world of the IT industry, where he can apply his technical expertise and contribute to innovative solutions.
